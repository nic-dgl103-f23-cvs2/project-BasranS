Lifeline Communicator Units

Our standard medical alert system helps you maintain your independence in and around the home. It quickly connects you to our response centre for 24/7 assistance with a simple push of your Lifeline help button.

Types of Communicators

A Regular Lifeline Communicator that is connected to an active telephone jack and electrical outlet.This type of unit allows you to connect your existing phone into back of the Lifeline unit so that your phone works through the Lifeline unit.
A Wireless Lifeline Communicator that works with it's own cellular technology; this unit is connected to an electrical outlet in your home.

Features

  • Press your Help Button anytime you need help.
  • Depending on a level of help needed we’ll call a neighbour, loved one or emergency services.
  • Simple setup and intuitive operation make this unit accessible for all ages. It seamlessly integrates into your home, adapting to your lifestyle.
  • In case of a fall or sudden impact, our system detects it and initiates a call for help if you're unable to do so yourself.

Personal Help Buttons

HomeSafe Basic

Wear the HomeSafe emergency button in the fashion that suits you. Choose from a medical alert pendant that is worn around the neck or a wristband that adjusts to fit your wrist.

  • Light weight
  • Button can be worn around the neck or wrist
  • Long lasting battery we change when needed
  • 100% waterproof

HomeSafe AutoAlert

HomeSafe AutoAlert is the only mobile medical alert device on the market that provide protection even while the unit is charging.
"AutoAlert does not detect 100% of falls, if able, you should always push your button if you need help".

  • Adjustable cord to wear around the neck
  • Fall detection technology
  • Help at the push of the button or an automatic signal sent when a fall is detected*
  • 100% waterproof
